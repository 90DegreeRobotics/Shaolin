# Daily Form — 日课 rìkè

One sheet per day. Page 1: the bells and the Daily Check-In.
Page 2: the Mandarin journal page. Fill it at the Evening Gate; write the
truth, not the flattering version. Blank fields stay blank.

Printable version: [print/daily_form.html](print/daily_form.html).
